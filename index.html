The Guile 100 Programs Project

So, we're writing a book. It is a reference on GNU Guile, an implementation of the Scheme computer language. It is a collection of solutions.

But, when I say we're writing a book, I mean us: you and me. I'm crowdsourcing a book. It'll be 100 examples, and I'll need your help.

Every few weeks is a new theme: examples on one topic. We'll explore Guile's ability solve a category of problems.

Each week is a new challenge in that theme: a new script to be written. If you volunteer, you have a couple of weeks to show us what you can do.

You don't have to be an expert. These shouldn't be hard problems.

I'm not asking you to do this just for honor. Most of the problems with have bounties for the completion of the task. Usually 100 USD.

This is an ambitious project, and I have no idea what will happen next.
